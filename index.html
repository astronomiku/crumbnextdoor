About us

Three friends fresh out of college, and a shared passion for baking. What began as a creative outlet between classes and exams has since grown into a small bakery business built on simple ingredients, reliable recipes, and a mutual love for pastry done right.

Each friend brings something unique to the table, but they all share a genuine appreciation for creating baked goods that taste just as good as they look.

From cookies and croissants to whatever new ideas they're experimenting with, quality, detail, and flavor are the top priorities — always.

Thanks for stopping by and supporting something small and growing.
